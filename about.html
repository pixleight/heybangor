About #HeyBangor

A little lunchtime hackathon project by Chris Violette

What's this all about?

Recently, I've been seeing the hashtag '#heybangor' popping up on Twitter and Facebook. I decided to put together quick & dirty site to collect some of these tweets and posts.

Why?

It's good practice, for one. Secondly, Bangor is growing, socially. Between the downtown revitalization, the growing art scene, and even us young folks making some big decisions for the city, Bangor needs as much of a voice as it can get. Keeping a finger on Bangor's pulse can't hurt.

And finally, it's a good lead-in for a larger project for the Bangor region I've been working on.

What's with this "fork me" nonsense? Some sort of innuendo?

If you're technically-inclined, I'm sharing the source code for this project on GitHub. You are free to "fork" the project (make a copy for yourself), make your own version, and even contribute back to my project.
